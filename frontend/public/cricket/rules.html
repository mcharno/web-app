Staff Casual Cricket League -- Rules 2010

  1. Each match to start at the stated time 5:30. Offending teams to forfeit the toss.
  2. Each innings to consist of 20 x 6 ball overs.
  3. Maximum number of overs by any one player is 4.
  4. Cricket boots or plimsolls must be worn
  5. 2 points for a win - 1 point per team if match is abandoned (though one rearrangement will be attempted) or tied (a match will only be tied if both teams have the same number of runs, wickets lost and balls bowled).
  6. A team captain who finds that he cannot field a team should contact the opposition captain and the Sports Centre (ext. 3855) as soon as possible.
  7. A team failing to turn up will forfeit the points.
  8. The first named team shall collect the equipment bag from the Sports Centre.
  9. Please take care of equipment - both sides will be held responsible and any damage should be reported to Reception on returning the bag after the match.
  10. If the grass wicket is unplayable due to rain, etc. it may be possible to use Artificial Wicket 2.  This is not well maintained and may need a bit of work to make it playable
    Please play the match if at all possible.
  11. If the groundstaff have called the game off then the points are shared and the game is not re scheduled. There are no blank dates in the fixture list in 2009
  12. If captains  are in disagreement the decision to abandon should prevail. The points are shared and the game will not be re scheduled.
  13. Players who do not normally play for a staff team and are NOT members of staff of the University or Smith and Nephew, nor Graduates  with a position on campus and who are brought in to make up numbers in a team are only allowed to bowl 2 overs maximum and bat at number 7 or below.  Players may only play for one team in the league each season. NO undergraduates may play in matches other than to act as substitute fielders.
  14. Each team should provide a match ball for the game (NOT NECESSARILY A NEW ONE)  and the  2 captains will decide which is the better. This one ball will be used for the whole game unless it gets lost. 
  15. Teams can arrange friendlies on the all weather strip. 
  16. In the event of 2 teams finishing top of the league with equal points if the league game has been played between the two then the head to head record will decide the winner otherwise a play off will be arranged to decide the Championship.
  17. Because of the shortage of spaces to re arrange games, EVERY opportunity should be made to play the game on the assigned date. Captains should not call games off  by phone at 3pm. Ideally both teams should meet on 22 Acres at 5.30 pm and make a decision then. Don't ring the Sports Centre. Go down and make the decision yourselves. If you want to play it's up to you to get the game played. Don't let team members  talk you into making a decision to cancel at 4 pm.
