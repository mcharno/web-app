19 July

PLONGE

119-4

PLONGE take the points but not without a few scares. Batting first they were reduced to 21-3 before Atkinson (60) and Watt (31) put on 81 together to allow them to reach 119 off 20 overs.  After losing Abbas for 6, Duscheiko and Anderson looked completely in control reaching 54 off 10 overs. Anderson was plumb LBW for 18 but Duscheiko continued choosing his shots carefully and after 2 straight sixes IRISS needed 28 off the last 4 overs. Then a terrible mix up allowed PLONGE to remove Duscheiko (54) run out by Atkinson with the score on 94. Donnelly bowled 2 tight overs and PLONGE were home.

We hope Townend (dislocated finger) is not detained in A+E too long!
