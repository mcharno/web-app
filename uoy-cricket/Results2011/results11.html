A final word from Dave:

Tonight it's a sad day for the league losing a whole clutch of graduates who have made the league their own over the last 3 years.
 Gul initially, then Mumtaz, Afaq, Nomi, Mudassar, Suleman, Adil and Salman have galvanised cricket at York.
Their enthusiasm for the game has made the league stronger and more difficult to win than ever before.
Their participation in saturday cricket made York staff the Vale league champions in 2009.
Most importantly in a white middle class city like York they have broken down barriers and made many friends with their courtesy, humour and infectious love of cricket.
I will miss them all enormously as will the league and I hope everyone has learnt as much from them as I have.

Hear! Hear!
