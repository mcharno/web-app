Mon.
16th May.
 PLONGE
  144-3
  PLONGE won the toss and batted first in constant drizzle scoring a very tidy 144-3 built around a solid unbeaten Atwell innings of 48no, Maltravers 21, and Waites (though not the pony tailed star of many Biology matches I'm informed) 24. All accompanied by a non stop commentary from Mendham behind the stumps. In reply Armitage went first ball LBW, but Mendham smashed a quickfire 51 and James 48. Allen the pick of the bowlers with 4-26. 18 off  5 overs and Panthers were left needing 12 from 2 overs. Levesley and Fazeel faced Waites.  Tight bowling, and 8 needed off last 6 balls. 5 came from 3 deliveries then Fazeel run out for 20. Dot ball and only  2 came from the final delivery leaving Panthers, as in their last game of 2010, a despairing run short. PHEW.

    Smith and Nephew XI
 110-2
 Psychos bat first and despite a carefully built half century from Prendergast (52), finally superbly caught by Coleman, and 15 from Simpson. The rest fall away to leave Psychos with a just defendable total. Vinigrad the pick of the bowlers with 3-16, Grundy (0-13) and Maudsley keeping things tight and only Milner (0-26) taking any stick. In reply there is only one result after Maudsley strikes a brutal 10 over 69, aided by Langton with 26. Moreland does his best with 2-16 but it's all over in 13 overs.

McCarthy does his best with 3-10 but the Bios power proves too much for the gaps in the field.
