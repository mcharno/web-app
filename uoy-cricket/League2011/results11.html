Thur 2nd June Physically Challenged Panthers 148-6 On a beautiful summer evening Panthers batted first and quickly got into trouble, losing the opening pair cheaply to be 14-2. Holmes (25) and Pratt (16) settled the ship somewhat, but when both went relatively cheaply things started to look good for Smith and Nephew. Fortunately for Panthers, Saeed took up the challenge and swung lustily at many a ball, connecting often enough to make a sterling 43. He was ably supported by Haider (39). Their middle order partnership, together with a strong performance by Sundries (16), allowed Panthers to notch up 148-6. Wickets were evenly shared by the S&N bowlers, one each for 5 of the 6 bowlers.
In reply S&N batted valiantly, with Milner top scoring on 27 and Hoskins making a useful 17, but the Panther bowling was too strong: Holmes 3-10, Gent 2-6, Ferguson 2-19. Nonetheless, S&N stayed the distance, finishing with 71-9.
The game was played in a very good spirit and enjoyed by all.

Mon 13th June Alcohology Old Boys XI  81a.o.
 A great start to the season from PDP XI. With Shailesh building a patient 53n.o. and Ankor 44 it was steady progress from 70-3 off the 1st ten overs. The 5 wickets were shared, with an excellent catch from Charno but nobody managed to slow down the rate.
